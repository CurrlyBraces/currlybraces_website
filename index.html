How it works

Set up an auto-generated portfolio

Developers need to share their work experience and coding skill-sets with us just for the first time and rest we take care of generating, maintaining and updating an infographic portfolio along with an AI based reputation score on the basis of their expertise.

Contribute to the community

All the developers of this platform can interact with each other by getting or providing personal assistance to solve technical queries, sharing valuable time & knowledge and improving their reputation score in return.

Get in the eyes of the clients

We recommend the profile of the developers on the basis of their reputation score to our elite clients. Once the profile is selected by the client, developers will get chance to work on the paid projects under the guidance of our project manager and also get an impact on the reputation score on the basis of their performance. Higher reputation score of a developer means higher chance of getting hired at higher rates.
